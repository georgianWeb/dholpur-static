Georgians Academy for Professional Studies (GAPS) was established on 1st April 2013 with a passion to motivate and guide the candidates in realising their dream of becoming an officer in the Armed Forces. In the last 8 years this institution has proved its credentials by giving best results in NDA/CDS written and SSB. Today we are considered the best institution for NDA/CDS written and SSB guidance in Delhi and one of the best institutions in India for SSB preparation.

GAPS is a dream project of Like-minded people who are alumni of "King George's Royal Indian Military College" (KGRIMC) presently known as Rastriya Military School, Ex Armed Forces Officers and Educationists. The sole aim of establishing this institution is to provide a platform to the students of Rastriya Military Schools, Sainik Schools, KV and Wards of Ex-army personnel where they can cherish their dream of wearing uniform and serve the country and the elite Defence Forces.

Our institute caters for the economically weaker section candidates by charging minimum fees for quality education and providing them the best opportunity to achieve success in written test and SSB. We believe that quality education and proper guidance is the right of every youngster. No youngster should be deprived of it just because he/she doesn't have resources to realise his/her dream.


Since April 2013 we have taken small strides towards realising bigger dreams. With three recommendations in 2013, seven in 2014, twelve in 2015, eighteen in 2016, twenty-seven in 2017, twenty-seven in 2018, forty-eight in 2019, seventy in 2020 and 28 in first six months of 2021 we have given 240 recommendations in SSB for various entries of Army, Navy and Airforce. Georgians Academy for professional studies feels proud in sharing that it has been able to transform the personality of thousands of students and contribute towards Nation building through these youngsters and budding scholar warriors.

World Sunrise Public School (GIS) is a dream project of GAPS. The basic aim of this project is to develop a feeder institution for National Defence Academy, Khadakwasla and its sister academies throughout the country. The school is spread over 20 Acres of land surrounded by lush green flora and fauna. It has a fully residential set up designed to develop the overall personality of the students and groom them into the future officers and leaders of the elite Armed Forces. The Campus has all the modern facilities which is required to make the living of students comfortable and secure. The school will provide an integrated 2-year program where students will follow CBSE curriculum of X+II and prepare for NDA written exam, groom their personalities simultaneously on lines of Military and Sainik Schools with the experience of Ex Officers who commanded them. The daily routine is designed to develop Officer like qualities (OLQs) in the students which will help them in clearing the SSB. We strive to make this project one of the most successful projects in the field of Defence preparation. Jai Hind
