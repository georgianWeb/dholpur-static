Georgians Academy for Professional Studies (GAPS) was established in April 2013 with a passion to motivate and guide the candidates in realising their dream of becoming an officer in the Armed Forces. In the last 7 years this institute has proved its credentials by giving best results in NDA/CDS written and SSB. Today the institute is considered Best for NDA coaching and SSB coaching in Delhi and one of the best coaching in India for SSB preparations.

GAPS is a dream project of Like-minded people who are alumni of "King George Royal Indian Military College"(KGRIMC) presently known as Rashtriya Military School, Ex Armed forces officers and Educationists. The sole aim of establishing this institution is to provide a Platform to the students of Rashtriya Military Schools, Sainik schools, KV and Wards of Ex-army personal where they can cherish their dream of wearing Uniform and serve the country.

Our institute caters for the economically weaker section candidates by charging minimum fees and providing them the best of opportunity to get success in written and SSB. Since April 2013 we have taken small strives towards bigger dream. With Three recommendations in 2013, seven in 2014, Twelve in 2015, eighteen in 2016, twenty seven in 2017, twenty seven in 2018, forty eight in 2019 and thirty seven in first three months of 2020, we have given 178 recommendations till April 2020.

Georgians Academy for professional studies feels proud in sharing that it has been able to transform the personality of hundreds of students and contribute towards National building through these youngsters.
